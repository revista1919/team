About

Juan Pablo Miralles is a third-year high school student at Calasanz School, primarily interested in mathematics and chemistry. He participates in the UC Talent Academy. He is a three-time team finalist in CMAT, a member of the 2025 winning team, and a two-time national mathematics finalist, a bronze medalist in the 2024 National Mathematics Olympiad (junior level), and a silver medalist in the 2025 ONM (senior level).

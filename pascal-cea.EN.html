About

Pascal Cea is a third-year high school student at Colegio Curimón in the San Felipe commune, deeply interested in politics and history. He stands out for his leadership, serving as president of his school's Student Council. He is also a regional finalist in the Delibera Tournament of the National Congress Library, a tournament he has participated in twice. Additionally, he has taken part in the "Closer Economy" competition of the Central Bank of Chile and the Central Bank of Chile's short story competition about pesos. He has also been a participant in the Transparency Tournament organized by the CPLT. He is a politics enthusiast, a great orator, and a lover of writing.
